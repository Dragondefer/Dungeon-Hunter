⚔️ Game Features

🏰

Procedural Dungeons

Explore endless, randomly generated dungeons with combat, treasure, shop, rest, puzzle, and boss rooms. Every playthrough is unique!

⚔️

Strategic Combat

Engage in turn-based battles with skills, critical hits, and stamina management. Plan your moves wisely to survive!

📈

Character Progression

Level up your character, choose class specializations, acquire new skills, and become more powerful with each adventure.

🎒

Inventory Management

Collect weapons, armor, potions, and magical items. Manage your inventory strategically to maximize your effectiveness.

📋

Quest System

Complete various quests with different objectives and earn valuable rewards to aid your dungeon exploration.

💾

Save System

Save your progress anytime and continue your adventure later. Multiple save slots available for different characters.
